As a student teaching assistant in the Pure Mathematics division at Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University, I was responsible for teaching MTH122 Abstract Algebra. Additionally, as an Academic Navigator in the School of Mathematics and Physics, I regularly conducted knowledge consolidation lectures and provided academic support to fellow students. Prior to these roles, I served as a lecturer for the XJTLU Mathematics Society, delivering algebra midterm and final review sessions for all mathematics majors.

I have always adhered to the learning philosophy that "the best way to truly understand a problem is to explain it clearly to others," and I have greatly benefited from this output-oriented approach to learning. You can explore all my undergraduate teaching engagements on this page.
